Loose Meals Ratings and Reviews

Listing of the top meals and restaurants showcased on Loose Meals. Ratings of the most enticing experiences with the most beautiful and delectable meals.

Javiers - La Jolla

(9.2/10) - Crispy and meaty taquitos, fresh salsa verde on top. Great appetizer to share, the tender meat and crunchy shell makes this meal fantastic. Located in UTC, this restaurant has a beautiful ambiance and tasty food.

Javiers

Jubba Restaurant - San Jose

(9.5/10) - Delicious Somali food, tender beef and exotic spices. Beef Suqaar served with Injera, the Injera was spongey and light yet had a great bite. Fresh complimentary tea served with your meal, this restaurant makes you feel like at a family members house. Great quality ingredients and specialty dishes.

Jubba Restaurant

Sea Salt Pho & Sushi - San Diego

(9.0/10) - Rare Steak with Brisket Pho and Sunshine Roll. Heart warming beef broth with juicy pieces of beef and light noodles. Fragrant herbs and spices with a delicate and enticing sushi roll. Located in 4s Commons, this small restaurant serves wonderful pho and sushi.

Sea Salt Pho & Sushi

In-N-Out - San Diego

(9.2/10) - One of California's best burger joints, In-N-Out serves up classic burgers with only the freshest ingredients. Consistent quality and taste, this burger never seems to disappoint. The texture and taste of the juicy beef with melty cheese and crunchy yellow chilis makes for a perfect bite. If you grew up in California, In-N-Out has a special place in your heart.

In-N-Out

Merrimans - Maui

One of Maui's top restaurants, Merrimans was a wonderful experience. (9.7/10) - Boasting a beautiful ambiance and view of the Maui ocean horizon, Merrimans captivated the eye. The multi-course meal was fantastic, there was fresh ahi tuna, delectable sides, and fresh steak.

Somi Somi - San Jose

Delicious ice cream with a light and airy nutella stuffed waffle fish (takayaki). (9.4/10) - Located across California, this dessert is bringing people from all over to try. They pair the ice cream with toppings and a warm filling of your choice.

Somi Somi

Japanese Tea Garden - Balboa Park

Fresh salmon with the best teriyaki sauce over a bed of rice. (10/10) - One of my favorite meals, when asked "What would you eat as a last meal?" I would respond with this delicious plate. A classic meal since childhood, this is one of the best meals in San Diego. A beautiful environment at Balboa Park with a view of the Japanese Tea Garden.

Japanese Tea Garden

The Stand - Calabasas

Some of the best hot dogs, The Stand packs their dogs with high quality meat and fresh ingredients. (8.8/10) - Great flavor profiles and gourmet style, these hot dogs were everything you want in a hot dog.

The Stand

Kiin Thai - Del Mar

This green curry was fragrant and herbal. (9.5/10) - It had perfect notes of thai basil and spice which complimented the chicken. On the Sky Deck in Del Mar, this restaurant has a great ambiance and beautiful plating.

Kiin Thai

Tacos - Tijuana

On the bustling streets of Tijuana, this taco shop was serving fresh prime quality tacos. (9.3/10) - Spitfire roasted meats, this taco shop sliced the meats over the fire onto your tortilla. The fresh guacamole was amazing and the salsas hit the spot.

TJ Tacos

Udon - Osaka

One of the primest meals of my life, this udon was an authentic and immersive experience. (9.8/10) - Delightful broth with succulent shrimp tempura and fresh ingredients. You sat down without chairs on low tables and ate the most delicious udon. In the heart of Dotonbori next to the river, this meal was a memory you cherish for the rest of your life.

Udon

Cocola Bakery - San Jose

Delightful mango cake with fresh cream and fruit. (9.1/10) - Located in Santana Row, this bakery is a little gem with amazing rare desserts. High quality ingredients and fresh batches.

Cocola Bakery

Kith Treats - New York

Crazy quality and tasty ice cream, infused with various cereals. (9.6/10) - This ice cream was a great dessert in SoHo. One of my favorite places to go in NYC for a look at the exotic shoes/clothes and to get a bite of some crazy treats.

Kith Treats

Wings N Things - San Diego

San Diego local spot, some of the best chicken wings around. (9.3/10) - Classic spot on a gameday, the chicken is juicy and crispy while the sauce is spicy and mouthwatering. Fresh breadsticks to pair with the wing sauce seals the deal.

Wings N Things

Fairfax gem, Sweet Chick serves some of the best chicken waffles known to humans. The various seasonings and crispy batter make it amazing, however the chicken is the star of the show. They pair it with housemade butters, berry butter, herbal butter, and honey nut butter.
